## Open Questions & Uncertainties

Document what you don't know:
- What remains unclear or unjustified?
- What needs verification or follow-up?
- What interpretations could be wrong?
- What contradicts the main hypothesis?
- What evidence would change conclusions?
- What are the blind spots?

List explicitly. Intellectual honesty is strength.
