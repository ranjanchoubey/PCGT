## Pattern Discovery from Experiments

Analyze trends across multiple experiments:
- What patterns do you observe?
- What consistently works or fails?
- What assumptions appear incorrect?
- What new hypotheses emerge?
- Where are surprises or contradictions?
- What is counterintuitive?

Be critical. Do NOT try to make results look good.
Focus on weaknesses and failures.
