## Major Weaknesses

Identify real limitations:
- Core weaknesses in approach
- Failure cases and edge cases
- Assumptions that break or are unjustified
- Experiments missing from validation
- Alternative interpretations of results
- Where evidence is weak

Be brutally honest. This is where papers get rejected or accepted.
